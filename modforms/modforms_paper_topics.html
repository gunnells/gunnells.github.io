Modular Forms (Math 797MF) Paper Topics

Here are some suggestions for topics for your paper. It’s not required to pick one of these; you can certainly pick something else if you like.

  • Modular forms and theta functions.

  • Ramanujan graphs and their applications.

  • Modular symbols.

  • The trace formula.

  • Quaternion algebras and compact quotients of the upper half plane.

  • The Langlands program.

  • Dimension formulas.

  • The Bruhat-Tits building and Hecke operators.

  • Siegel modular forms.

  • Hilbert modular forms.

  • Bianchi modular forms.

  • The modularity theorem and elliptic curves.
